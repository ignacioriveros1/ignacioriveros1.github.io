## Welcome!
I’m Ignacio Riveros, a data scientist at Instagram. I received my Ph.D. in Quantitative Marketing from the [USC Marshall School of Business](https://www.marshall.usc.edu){:target="_blank"}, where I was advised by [Lan Luo](https://faculty.marshall.usc.edu/Lan-Luo/){:target="_blank"}, [Davide Proserpio](https://www.marshall.usc.edu/personnel/davide-proserpio){:target="_blank"}, and [Stephanie Tully](https://www.marshall.usc.edu/personnel/stephanie-tully){:target="_blank"}.
My research lies at the intersection of marketing, public policy, and political economy. I use econometric and causal inference tools to understand how consumers respond to firm behavior and policy changes—especially in politically or technologically complex environments.
